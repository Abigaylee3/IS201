Mental Health Awareness

About Mental Health

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

Why Mental Health Matters

  • Good mental health is essential to overall health and well-being.
  • It enables us to cope with the normal stresses of life, work productively, and contribute to our communities.
  • Mental health problems, such as depression, anxiety, and stress, are common and can affect anyone.
  • Seeking help and support is crucial for recovery.
